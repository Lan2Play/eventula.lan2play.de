
Developer Meeting
==================================================

We host a developer meeting on first tuesday all three months at 20:00 MEZ/MESZ on our teamspeak server lan2play.de .
You are welcome to join uns and discuss about the project, give feedback or just listen to the discussion.

You can find and propose the topics for the next meeting in our github repository at, where you also can find the protocol of the last meetings:
https://github.com/Lan2Play/eventula-dev-meetings
